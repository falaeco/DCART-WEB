Description English:

In the design studio, Unbound in Perfect Print, students were tasked with independently developing several books, and were responsible for design & layout, content creation, print, and assembly. In March 2019, I conceived Something About to Happen, a short book that explored and brought awareness to the conditions we subject ourselves to while living in the suburbs, and the significant environmental and social implications of rapidly expanding suburban sprawl. The book consisted of a short essay, as well as an extensive photographic collection that attempts to communicate the more abstruse emotional impact the suburbs have on its inhabitants. 

----

Description French:

Dans le studio de design, Unbound in Perfect Print, les étudiants étaient chargés de développer indépendamment plusieurs livres, et étaient responsables de la conception et de la mise en page, de la création du contenu, de l’impression et de l’assemblage. En mars 2019, j’ai conçu Something About to Happen, un petit livre qui explorait et sensibilisait aux conditions auxquelles nous nous soumettons en vivant dans les banlieues, et aux importantes implications environnementales et sociales de l’étalement urbain en pleine expansion. Le livre se composait d’un court essai, ainsi que d’une vaste collection de photographies qui tente de communiquer l’impact émotionnel plus abstrus que les banlieues ont sur leurs habitants.
